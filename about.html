History

Rentals website is a website that specializes in helping people with need of finding rental houses to stay in. This website was first created in January 2022 and has helped thousand of people find rental houses at their own prices. This website was created with the aim of helping house hunters easily survey and choose from a list of rentals provided the best that suits them. At first, the rentals covered were few owing to the few number of clients demanding our services. But recently, we've expanded our coverage from one to three geographical areas(three counties) due to the high demand by our clients. We have always strived to deliver the best services to our clients and ensure each one gets the best service delivery.
